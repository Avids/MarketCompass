Accumulation/Distribution (A/D) LineAccumulation/Distribution (A/D) Guide:
• Upward A/D Trend: Buying pressure (accumulation). Institutions are building positions.
• Downward A/D Trend: Selling pressure (distribution). Institutions are liquidating.
• Bullish Divergence: Price makes lower lows while A/D makes higher lows (strong reversal warning).
• Bearish Divergence: Price makes higher highs while A/D makes lower highs.
